EPSTEIN HUNT

About the Game: Epstein Hunt is the ultimate campus-wide challenge that turns networking into a high-stakes race! Participants, either as individuals or teams, must scour the college grounds to find a person whose name begins with each letter of the alphabet. To keep things unpredictable, the A–Z checklist will be displayed on the official website in a completely randomized order, forcing you to think fast and move even faster. Your mission is to identify one person for every single letter, verify their name, and clear your board before anyone else. But keep your eyes peeled—the campus holds hidden surprises and "Easter Eggs" that could change the game at any moment. Full rules and these secret twists will be revealed right before the start, so stay sharp and get ready to hunt!
